  • Ground rules

    "us", "our", "we" refers to the Ice Generator service and its staff members.

  • Third-party services

    We use restorecord as a way for our users to verify in order to prevent alternate accounts, and provides us with information about the new users using our service. The privacy policy for RestoreCord can be found here: https://restorecord.com/privacy

  • What we collect

    The information we collect from our users includes:

    • IP Address
    • Information Discord gives out through its API (Includes your user ID, username, etc.)

  • What we use your information for

    We at Ice Generator wants to keep things safe for everyone. We use the information Discord gives us through its API to store information about your user to give you a better experience Your IP address is used for preventing the re-execution of certain tasks by the same user.

  • How we store your data

    The data is stored in a private linux server located in Dubai which is very secure. We update all the third-party services we use in order to stay up-to-date with the security fixes.

  • Underaged users

    The service we operate on (Discord) does not allow underaged users. We will never knowingly collect data from a person under 13 years old.

  • Your rights

    You have the right to know what data we hold about you and to request its deletion at any time. Just reach out to our administrators for assistance.





    • If you have any concerns or questions regarding your data, please don't hesitate to contact us. Your privacy matters to us, and we appreciate your trust in us.
